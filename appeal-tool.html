Step 0 — Know Your Violation
Which Violation Did You Receive?
TikTok issues 6 categories of violations for health & supplement content. Each requires a different appeal strategy. Identify yours before you write a single word.
CRITICAL 🏥
Health / Medical Claims
Content stated or implied the product diagnoses, treats, cures, or prevents a disease or medical condition. Most common for supplement creators.
"treats anxiety" "cures insomnia" "prevents disease" "fixes PCOS"
CRITICAL ⚗️
Regulated Goods Policy
Selling or promoting dietary supplements without proper seller authorization or required lab certifications. TikTok Shop requires invite-only approval for supplement sellers.
Missing lab reports cGMP not on file No FDA registration
HIGH ⚖️
Misleading / Deceptive Claims
Unsubstantiated claims about results, before/after comparisons, exaggerated timeframes, or claims that can't be backed by scientific evidence.
"lose 10lbs in 7 days" Before/after "guaranteed results"
HIGH 📜
Community Guidelines — Safety
General content policy violations: promoting unsafe practices, discouraging medical consultation, or claims that could endanger viewer health.
"stop taking your meds" Anti-medical Unsafe dosage
MEDIUM 🏷️
Branded Content / Disclosure
Paid partnership content not properly disclosed. TikTok requires branded content disclosure when creators receive payment, free products, or other compensation.
Missing #ad No disclosure toggle Hidden sponsorship
MEDIUM 👻
Shadowban / Reach Suppression
No official strike — video stays up but FYP distribution is throttled. Usually triggered by borderline language, restricted hashtags, or repeated policy-adjacent content.
Views collapsed FYP disappeared No official notice

How to Submit This Appeal
    If Your First Appeal Is Denied
    Second Appeal Strategy
    You only get two appeals per violation — and the second is your last chance. Most creators re-submit the same letter and get rejected again. Here's exactly what to change.

    Why First Appeals Get Rejected

    • ✗Generic, copy-paste language that doesn't address the specific violation
    • ✗Emotional tone ("This is so unfair", "I didn't do anything wrong")
    • ✗Wrong policy cited — appealing under the wrong category
    • ✗No supporting documentation attached
    • ✗Admitting to language that wasn't actually used
    • ✗Not explaining how the content differs from what's prohibited
    • ✗Spelling errors and poor formatting
    • ✗Submitting before gathering evidence
    • ✗Multiple appeals for the same content

    What to Add in Your Second Appeal

    • ✓Reference the first appeal decision explicitly and note what changed
    • ✓Add new or updated scientific substantiation for every claim
    • ✓Include lab reports, COAs, cGMP, FDA registration if not in first appeal
    • ✓Quote TikTok's own policy language and show compliance point by point
    • ✓Provide a timestamped video transcript highlighting safe framing
    • ✓If the claim was spoken, explain why it's experiential not diagnostic
    • ✓Document account history (length, engagement, prior compliance)
    • ✓State explicit remediation steps you've taken since first appeal
    • ✓Be more specific — name the exact timestamp and exact phrase at issue

    When Appeals Are Exhausted
    Escalation Roadmap
    The in-app appeal process has exactly two levels. If both fail, here are the remaining paths — in order of effectiveness.
    Level 1
    In-App First Appeal
    Navigate to Creator Health Rating → Violations → select the violation → Appeal (at bottom of page). Must be submitted within 30 days of the violation notice. This is your strongest option — go in with all evidence ready.
    ⏱ 30 days to file ⏱ 7 business days for decision 📱 In-app
    Level 2
    In-App Second Appeal
    Same path: Creator Health Rating → Violations → Appeal. Must be filed within 15 calendar days of first denial. This is final — include everything. New evidence is mandatory; re-submitting the same content guarantees rejection.
    ⏱ 15 days after first denial ⏱ 7 business days for decision 📱 In-app
    Level 3
    TikTok Business Center / Creator Support
    After both appeals are exhausted, email business-services@tiktok.com with a formal written request referencing both appeal IDs. This is not an official appeal channel — it is a formal complaint. Outcome is at TikTok's discretion and not guaranteed, but it has worked for creators with agency relationships.
    ⏱ No set deadline 📧 business-services@tiktok.com
    Level 4
    Brand / Agency TikTok Contact
    If your brand or MCN has a dedicated TikTok account manager or partnership contact, escalate through them. TikTok treats partner-escalated cases differently from individual creator submissions. This is especially relevant for TikTok Shop sellers with high GMV accounts.
    Requires existing partnership Contact your TikTok rep
    Level 5 — Last Resort
    New Account + Strict Compliance Restart
    After two denied appeals, if the account is permanently restricted, TikTok's own guidance is to create a new account and strictly adhere to all community guidelines from day one. Before restarting: audit all planned content against this tool's pre-post checklist. Do not transfer banned content to the new account.
    ⏱ Immediate (after all appeals exhausted) Fresh start strategy
    Reference — Language Rules
    Safe vs. Banned Language
    For magnesium and ashwagandha supplements specifically. Spoken and written language both trigger moderation — the same rules apply to captions, voiceover, and on-screen text overlays.

    🚫 Banned — Instant Flag

    • ✗"Treats anxiety / depression / stress disorder"
    • ✗"Cures insomnia / sleep disorder"
    • ✗"Reduces cortisol levels" (medical measurement claim)
    • ✗"Fixes hormonal imbalance / PCOS / perimenopause"
    • ✗"Like Xanax / Valium / Ambien" (pharma comparison)
    • ✗"Clinically proven to" (without clinical trial evidence on file)
    • ✗"Stop taking your prescription and try this"
    • ✗"Guaranteed to work within X days"
    • ✗"Cures brain fog / ADHD / cognitive decline"
    • ✗"Lowers blood pressure / blood sugar"

    ✅ Safe — Use These Instead

    • ✓"I feel like I sleep better since I started taking this"
    • ✓"Supports relaxation and a calm nervous system"
    • ✓"Helps maintain healthy magnesium levels"
    • ✓"I noticed I feel less wound up in the evenings"
    • ✓"Designed to support occasional stress"
    • ✓"Contributes to normal muscle function"
    • ✓"I feel more rested when I take this consistently"
    • ✓"Helps me unwind at the end of the day"
    • ✓"Supports mental clarity and focus" (general wellness)
    • ✓"Not medical advice — talk to your doctor" (disclaimer)

TikTok Compliance
Stay Safe — Every. Single. Video.
One wrong word can get your video removed and your account flagged. Know the rules before you film.

🚫 Never Say These

  • instant bancures / treats / heals
  • instant bantestosterone booster
  • instant banfat burner
  • instant banhormonal treatment / cures hormones
  • instant banfixes anxiety / depression
  • shadowbanbefore and after
  • shadowbanguaranteed results
  • shadowbanlose weight fast

✅ Always Say These Instead

  • safemay support / may help
  • safesupports healthy cortisol balance
  • safein my personal experience
  • safeI noticed / I personally felt
  • safestudies suggest / research shows
  • safenot medical advice
  • saferesults may vary
  • safesupports hormonal balance
Quick Safe Swaps
❌ Banned✅ Safe Swap
boosts testosteronesupports your body's natural testosterone production
cures anxietymay support a calm, balanced nervous system
fixes your hormonessupports healthy hormonal balance
treats insomniamay support deeper, more restful sleep
burns fatsupports healthy metabolism and energy balance
this will make you feel betterin my experience, I noticed…
Visual Rules
🚫
No ingestion on camera — Don't show yourself putting the gummy in your mouth or swallowing. Hold it up instead.
🚫
No before/after — Side-by-side body comparisons are an instant removal. No exceptions.
🚫
No medical settings — Avoid filming in or near hospitals, clinics, or wearing anything that reads as medical.
✅
Always show the label — Close-up of the ingredient label builds trust and shows transparency.
✅
Add disclaimer text overlay — "Not medical advice. Results may vary." on screen AND in the caption — every video.
✅
Use lifestyle settings — Gym, kitchen, bedroom, morning routine. Natural, relatable environments.
Pre-Post Checklist — Run This Every Time
No banned words used (cures, treats, boosts testosterone, fat burner)
Used "may support" / "in my experience" framing throughout
Disclaimer text overlay on screen: "Not medical advice"
Disclaimer in caption too
No ingestion on camera (hold product, don't eat it)
No before/after visuals or body comparisons
3–5 hashtags max — no banned hashtags (#weightloss, #testosteronebooster)
Product label visible in at least one shot

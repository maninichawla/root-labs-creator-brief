3 The Journey Method — Before/After Done Right
⚠️ Before/After THUMBNAILS are what trigger flags — a dramatic bald-to-full-hair image in the thumbnail, even if the video is compliant. The video content itself (a personal timeline) is safer.
Creators can show hair changes over time — but it must be framed as a personal experience timeline, not a "product caused this transformation" comparison.
${[['📸 Day 1','Dry, dull scalp. Starting my hair wellness routine.'],['📸 Week 2','Scalp feels less dry. Noticing the product absorbs well.'],['📸 Week 4','Hair looks a bit fuller to me — may just be hydration.'],['📸 60 Days','My overall hair health feels improved. Results are mine personally.']].map(([label,text])=>`
${label.split(' ')[0]}
${label.split(' ').slice(1).join(' ')}
${text}
`).join('')}
🚫 Thumbnail Don'ts
  • Split-screen bald vs. full hair
  • Dramatic before/after side-by-side
  • Overlaid text: "REGREW HAIR IN 30 DAYS"
  • Extreme close-up of sparse vs. thick scalp
✅ Thumbnail Dos
  • Creator holding or applying the product
  • Lifestyle shot with hair looking healthy
  • Text: "My 60-Day Hair Journey" (neutral)
  • Product flat-lay or ingredient focus
